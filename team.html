Meet The Team

Managing Partner, Chris Elbring,

Elbring is an information security consulting industry veteran with roots in developing information security software and hardware products.

He has managed elite consulting teams delivering guaranteed results with over $20 million (US) in annual revenue.

He has been behind some of the most cutting edge research in information security including running a group that had 14 presentations at BlackHat/Defcon in a single year.

Elbring was the featured speaker at a Congressional Briefing on automobile hacking and the Jeep hack (Miller/Valasek).

He built and ran a hardware/semiconductor reverse engineering lab whose equipment included a Reactive Ion Etcher, a chemical lab, a probing station, a Scanning Electron Microscope, and a Focused Ion Beam workstation.

Anvil Partner, Vincent Berg

Berg is a highly respected information security professional who has worked both as an independent consultant and as a Director of Services for one of the leading research driven information security firms.

In addition to serving as a highly technical Managing Consultant and a Principal Consultant, Berg has been a developer on a successful commercial startup in the Netherlands whose product is doing great thirteen years later.

Berg thoroughly understands SDLC, code review and pen testing from both sides of the aisle.

Berg is a trusted advisor for many well respected product teams and Fortune 100 organizations and is regularly requested by name to help and build out information security programs for development organizations.

Berg has been a code auditor on multiple operating system release teams and has run hundreds of projects. He has released research that was acclaimed by Bruce Schneier and found hundreds of industry impacting vulnerabilities.

He continues to be a highly technical team member/manager and still performs technical work for Anvil.
