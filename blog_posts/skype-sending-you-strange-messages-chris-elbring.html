SKYPE SENDING YOU STRANGE MESSAGES?

Published on November 16, 2016 Journal, Photography, Web Design 5 Comments 16

Here is my PSA for the week. In the last couple weeks I have gotten 5 messages from random people (folks I no longer communicate with on skype but was connected to at some point:and most of them in infosec) with links that look like this:

If you receive a message like that, don’t click the link. Delete the message. Find an alternate form of communication with whomever sent you the message – email, phone, text, facebook, twitter dm or something and let them know that they suck at passwords and need to change their password for Skype, specifically. And if they are naïve enough to use the same password somewhere else, change that as well.

The majority of these appear to have the link baidu.com but I have seen others as well. If your skype account sends me this message, I will likely just remove you from my skype contacts. There also appear to be a rash of these in information security right now. So be extra vigilant.

For my conspiracy theory level infosec friends, yes I know and please do not ask me why I still use skype sometimes.

Have a safe day!!!!!
